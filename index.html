About

AJ Graphic and Stationery Company is a dynamic entity offering a comprehensive suite of world-class stationery and graphic services. Established in Bardera in 2017, our rapid expansion has established us as a trusted partner, serving clients in four districts of the Gedo region of Somalia. Our commitment to excellence, innovation and unwavering dedication has cemented our reputation as an industry leader.
